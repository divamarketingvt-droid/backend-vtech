How Identity Verification Works

A seamless, secure process to deliver accurate, fast and compliant identity —without friction for you or your candidates.

1

Secure ID Submission

Candidates upload their government-issued ID—Aadhar, PAN, or Passport or Voter ID - through an end to end encrypted web portal or mobile, ensuring the data privacy from the start.

2

Intelligent Data Extraction

Advanced Secure OCR technology to captures the structures key details from the document with 99% of high accuracy, eliminating manual errors and speeding up the process.

3

Real-Time Database Validation

Extracted information is instantly verified against authorized government databases, ensuring the identity is valid, active, and compliant.

4

Biometric Identity Matching

Facial recognition technology securely matches the candidate’s live selfie photo with the ID photo, by using the live detection to eliminate impersonation risks.

5

Instant Verification Report

A comprehensive report is generated in seconds—highlighting authenticity status, risk indicators, and a tamper-proof verification certificate for audit readiness.
